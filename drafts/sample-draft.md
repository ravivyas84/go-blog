This is a sample draft post. It will not appear in the sitemap, RSS feed, post listings, or tag pages.

## Why Drafts?

Drafts allow you to share work-in-progress posts with specific people via a cryptic URL, without exposing them to the general public or search engines.

## How It Works

- Place your markdown file in the `drafts/` folder
- The build generates a cryptic URL like `/drafts/a1b2c3d4e5f6g7h8/`
- The post shows a visible "DRAFT" banner to readers
- Search engines are told not to index it via `noindex` meta tag
- The post is excluded from the sitemap, RSS feed, and all listing pages
